I am a cello performance student at Coe College with over 15 years of classical training in cello and violin. My work bridges performance and the music industry, with experience in music production, studio operations, and arts administration through internships at Electric Lady Studios, Tanglewood, and Orchestra Iowa. I am passionate about contributing to creative projects in production, artist development, and music management.

I have developed hands-on experience in artist management and client relations through work in both studio and performance settings. At Electric Lady Studios, I supported sessions involving high-profile artists, gaining exposure to VIP client services, studio coordination, and professional production environments. As an Artist Liaison at the Boston University Tanglewood Institute (BUTI), I managed artist needs, coordinated schedules, and ensured a seamless experience for performers throughout the program. I also contributed to contract and talent coordination at Cedar Rapids Opera, supporting administrative processes and artist logistics. These roles have strengthened my ability to work directly with artists, manage details in fast-paced environments, and contribute to high-level creative operations.
